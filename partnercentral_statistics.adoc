[.lead]
The Statistics screen shows statistics for 8 categories: 

* *Licensed Customers*
* *Trials (Customers)*
* *Active Jobs*
* *Export Jobs*
* *Protected Users*
* *Restore Jobs*
* *Total Customers*
* *Total Jobs*Two boxes for Customers and Jobs appear below the statistics categories. After you add customers, graphs appear in these two boxes.
